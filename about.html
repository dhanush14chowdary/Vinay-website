About BMS College of Commerce & Management

B.M.S. College of Commerce & Management (BMSCCM) is run under the aegis of BMS Educational Trust, Bengaluru. The college is affiliated to Bangalore City University, Bengaluru and offers programs as per the prescribed syllabus of the university. BMSCCM is centrally located in the verdant green environs of V.V Puram, Basavanagudi Bengaluru.

Programs Offered

The college offers undergraduate courses in Commerce, Management, and Computer Applications Programs, and an Executive Management Program for working professionals.

Facilities and Infrastructure

  • Modern air-conditioned classrooms
  • State-of-the-art computer labs
  • Sports and recreation centre
  • Administrative block
  • Acoustically modeled seminar hall
  • Library and information centre

Awards and Recognitions

  • India’s Top Higher Education Jury Award 2022 as 50 Best Private B-School by Education Today in October 2022
  • Dewang Mehta Award and Jury Award for Most Prominent Education Brand for Commerce & Management College presented by ET Ascent in November 2022
  • Most Promising and Leading Commerce and Management College of the Year in 2021, Karnataka awarded by BEGINUP Research Intelligence

Academic Excellence and Career Support

The Corporate Communication Department of the College offers career enhancement courses over and above the university curriculum which makes students industry-ready and employable. The College has a track record of 100% placements for the students. The highest salary package offered to a UG student in 2021-23 was Rs. 10,00,000/- PA. The students of the college are placed in all sectors of the industry.
